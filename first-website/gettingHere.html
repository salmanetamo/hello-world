JOIN US

We are located in the North of the Island, in the cosmopolite town Grand Baie. So it's pretty easy to find us whether you are using public transport or driving yourself. An even easier way to get here is to contact us and use our Pick-up facility at very cheap prices.

How to find us

On Grand Baie beach coastal road, we are 200m away from the Grand Baie police station(yeah, it's very safe here). At the police station, a billboard should be able to guide to our exact location. Or you could just ask around, anyone will be willing to help.
