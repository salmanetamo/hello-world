IT'S ALL PART OF THE ADVENTURE

At the Lilas Hostel, our first priority is the comfort of our clients. Well, we guarantee that comfort by providing a set of facilities that will allow you to relax as you explore the beautiful island. When we say relax, we mean relaaax.

Lilas Facilities

SWIMMING POOL

Mauritius is a tropical island, and it can get pretty hot sometimes. And one thing we can tell you is that after you visit some of the beautiful places that are here, you would probably want to cool yourself down and re-energize your body. Well, we got your back with this swimming pool that is at your disposal anytime. It is well taken care of, and we always make sure we follow all the necessary hygiene policies.



Service Hours: 8AM to 7PM
Service Charge: 10$

SPA

Are you back from hiking Le Pouce Mountain? Or maybe you are just back from your errands around the island and you are feeling very tired. Well, there is nothing better than taking a moment for yourself and get some soothing massage to reinvigorate your body. Our spa is there just for that to make sure you are always relaxed and ensuring your comfort is at its optimum.






Service Hours: 9AM to 12AM
Service Charge: 12$

BAR

You can't imagine a hostel without a bar, neither can we. That's why we have this bar at your disposal to make sure you never run out of your favorite drinks. The Bar is also a place for you to meet the people you are sharing the hostel with and also meet some Mauritians. After all, some of the best relationships are built after a drink or two with a complete stranger.


Service Hours: 6PM to 12AM
Service Charge: On order

GYM

You are a sporty person, and you want to make sure you do not disrupt your work out program. Well, don't worry; we got your back with the necessary equipment to keep your training going. So, you can hit the gym as much as you want, it's all there waiting for you.








Service Hours: 4PM to 7PM
Service Charge: 8$

PICK-UP

You are new to the country, and you want to make sure you have transport at your disposal at reasonable prices. Moving around the island can be challenge if you don't know your way around it. So, you can rent our rent cars to take you to or pick you from wherever you are on the island. And you have all of that at fairly cheap prices.







Service Hours: 24h/7
Service Charge: On order
